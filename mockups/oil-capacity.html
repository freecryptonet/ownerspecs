Service bulletin — 1.5 T oil dilution

Early 1.5 T units (model years 2016 to mid-2018) sold in cold climates exhibited fuel dilution of the engine oil. Honda issued software updates (PCM reprogramming, A18-076 and A19-014) that revised cold-start enrichment and shortened the recommended drain interval to 3,750 miles under severe-duty operation for affected VIN ranges.[5][6]

If the dipstick reads above the upper mark and the oil smells of fuel, the affected VIN range and updated drain interval likely applies. Dealer reprogramming is free under the Honda Maintenance Minder revision.
